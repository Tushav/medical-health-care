About Us

Medical and Health Care Assist was developed with ideas and help of leading professionals in the healthcare domain in Mauritius, ensuring that patients receive the highest quality medical care. As a result of using our services, our patients are able to stay in their homes longer, save money, and have peace of mind.

We bring quality medical care into our patients' homes and aim to make primary healthcare not only more accessible, but also more affordable and accountable to our patients' needs. Our team is a panel of experienced, registered doctors and medical professionals. We together guarantee the best and quality medical service.

We are a Home healthcare service in Mauritius providing services which consists of 24/7 Home doctor Consultation and Follow ups across the island.

We provide home visits for most cases and for all ages. Follow ups of patients with chronic conditions. A facility specially for bed ridden patients, children, tourists or ones with no transport or simply for ones who don't want to travel.
